📅 Fundraiser Days

Configure each day of Love Notes week. Add one row per performance day, in order. The Weekday must match the sheet name in your availability matrix exactly. The Responses Column is the 0-indexed column in your Google Form responses spreadsheet that holds the time slot for that day. Start/End Hour sets the performance window for that specific day (24-hour format — e.g. 8 = 8:00 AM, 21 = slots end by 9:00 PM). The Slack Intro is the opening line of the Slack announcement sent the night before.

💡 Update this table every year: change the dates, adjust start/end hours if needed, update column indices if your Google Form order changed, and edit the Slack intro lines to your liking.
